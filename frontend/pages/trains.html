Train Travel Tips

Arrive Early

Reach the station at least 30 minutes before departure to avoid last-minute rush.

Pack Light

Travel with minimal luggage for a more comfortable journey and easier storage.

Carry ID Proof

Always carry the ID proof used during booking for verification during the journey.

Food Options

Pre-book meals or carry snacks for long journeys to ensure you stay comfortable.
